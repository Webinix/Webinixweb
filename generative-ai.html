What is Generative AI?

Generative AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that can create new content—text, images, code, audio, video—based on patterns learned from training data. Unlike traditional AI that classifies or predicts, generative AI produces original outputs that didn't exist before. This includes technologies like GPT-4 for text, DALL-E for images, Copilot for code, and many more transformative applications.
